| Title        | Full faith and credit for public acts, etc.                                                 |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

Full faith and credit shall be given throughout Pakistan to public acts and records, and judicial proceedings of every Province.
