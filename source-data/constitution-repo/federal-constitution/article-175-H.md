(1) If, at any time, the President considers that it is desirable to obtain the opinion of the Federal
Constitutional Court on any question of law which he considers of public
importance, he may refer the question to the Federal Constitutional
Court for consideration.

(2) The Federal Constitutional Court shall consider a question so
referred and report its opinion on the question to the President.
