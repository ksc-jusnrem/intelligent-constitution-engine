(1) The Federal Constitutional Court shall consist of a Chief Justice to be known
as the Chief Justice of the Federal Constitutional Court of Pakistan and so
many other Judges as may be determined by Act of Majlis-e-Shoora
(Parliament) or, until so determined, as may be fixed by the President:
Provided that the Federal Constitutional Court may have equal
number of Judges from each Province.

(2) The President may, by Order, make necessary provisions for
the matters consequential or ancillary to the establishment and
functioning of the Federal Constitutional Court.
