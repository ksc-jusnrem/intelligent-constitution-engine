| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 13 November 2025                                                                            |

(1) The permanent seat of the Supreme Court shall, subject to clause (3), be at Islamabad.

(2) The Supreme Court may from time to time sit in such other places as the Chief Justice of Supreme Court, with the approval of the President, may appoint.
