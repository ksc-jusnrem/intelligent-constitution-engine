| Title        | Review of judgments or orders by the Federal Constitutional Court |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 13 November 2025 |
| Last Updated | 13 November 2025 |
| Source       | https://www.senate.gov.pk/uploads/documents/1762598611_995.pdf |
| Amendment 27 | https://www.senate.gov.pk/uploads/documents/1762598611_995.pdf |

The Federal Constitutional Court shall have the
power, subject to the provisions of any Act of Majlis-e-Shoora
(Parliament) and any rules made by the Federal Constitutional Court, to
review any judgment pronounced or any order made by it.
