| Title        | President's power to grant pardon, etc.                                                     |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

The President shall have power to grant pardon, reprieve and respite, and to remit, suspend or commute any sentence passed by any court, tribunal or other authority.
