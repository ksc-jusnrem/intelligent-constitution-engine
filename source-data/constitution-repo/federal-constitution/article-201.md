| Title        | Decision of High Court binding on subordinate courts.                                       |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

Subject to Article 189, any decision of a High Court shall, to the extent that it decides a question of law or is based upon or enunciates a principle of law, be binding on all courts subordinate to it.
