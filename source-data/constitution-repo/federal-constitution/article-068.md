| Title        | Restriction on discussion in Parliament.                                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 13 November 2025                                                                            |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |
| Amendment 27 | https://www.senate.gov.pk/uploads/documents/1762598611_995.pdf |

No discussion shall take place in Parliament with respect to the conduct of any Judge of the Supreme Court or of the Federal Constitutional Court, a High Court in the discharge of his duties.
