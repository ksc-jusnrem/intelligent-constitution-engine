| Title        | Power of Council to enforce attendance of persons, etc.                                     |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 13 November 2025                                                                            |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

(1) For the purpose of inquiring into any matter, the Council shall have the same power as the the Federal Constitutional Court or the Supreme Court has to issue directions or orders for securing the attendance of any person or the discovery or production of any document; and any such direction or order shall be enforceable as if it had been issued by the the Federal Constitutional Court or the Supreme Court.
