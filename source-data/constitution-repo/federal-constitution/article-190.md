| Title        | Action in aid of Federal Constitutional Court and Supreme Court.                            |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 13 November 2025                                                                            |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

All executive and judicial authorities throughout Pakistan shall act in aid of the Federal Constitutional Court and the Supreme Court.
