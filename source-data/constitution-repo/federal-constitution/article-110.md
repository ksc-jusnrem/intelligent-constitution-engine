| Title        | Right of Governor to address Provincial Assembly.                                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

The Governor may address the Provincial Assembly and may for that purpose require the attendance of the members.
