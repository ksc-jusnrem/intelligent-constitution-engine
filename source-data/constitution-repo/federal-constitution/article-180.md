| Title        | Acting Chief Justice.                                                                       |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 13 November 2025                                                                            |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

| Amendment 27 | https://www.senate.gov.pk/uploads/documents/1762598611_995.pdf |

At any time when—
(a) the office of Chief Justice of the Supreme Court is vacant ;
or
(b) the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court is absent or is unable to perform the functions of his office due to any other cause,

the President shall appoint one of the Judges of the Supreme Court who have not previously held the office of Chief Justice of the Supreme Court otherwise than under this Article to act as Chief Justice of the Supreme Court.
