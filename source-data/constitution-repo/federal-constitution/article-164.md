| Title        | Grants out of Consolidated Fund.                                                            |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

The Federation or a Province may make grants for any purpose, notwithstanding that the purpose is not one with respect to which Parliament or, as the case may be, a Provincial Assembly may make laws.
