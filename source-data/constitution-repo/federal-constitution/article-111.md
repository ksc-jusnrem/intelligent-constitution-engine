| Title        | Right to speak in Provincial Assembly.                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 21 October 2024                                                                             |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |
| Amendment 26 | https://www.na.gov.pk/uploads/documents/671f74b8da9e0_263.pdf |

The Advocate-General and an Adviser appointed under clause (11) of Article 130 shall have the right to speak and otherwise take part in the proceedings of the Provincial Assembly or any committee thereof of which he may be named a member, but shall not by virtue of this Article be entitled to vote.
