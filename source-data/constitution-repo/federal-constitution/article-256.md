| Title        | Private armies forbidden.                                                                   |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

No private organisation capable of functioning as a military organisation shall be formed, and any such organisation shall be illegal.
