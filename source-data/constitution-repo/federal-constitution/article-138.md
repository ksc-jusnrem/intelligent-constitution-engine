| Title        | Conferring of functions on subordinate authorities.                                         |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

On the recommendation of the Provincial Government, the Provincial Assembly may by law confer functions upon officers or authorities subordinate to the Provincial Government.
