| Title        | Protection of minorities.                                                                   |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf |

The State shall safeguard the legitimate rights and interests of minorities, including their due representation in the Federal and Provincial services.
