| Title        | Person acting in office not to be regarded as successor to previous occupant of office, etc. |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                               |
| Last Updated | 14 August 1973                                                                               |
| Source       | https://factfocus.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/Original-Constitution-of-1973-Pakistan.pdf  |

For the purposes of the Constitution, a person who acts in an office shall not be regarded as the successor to the person who held that office before him or as the predecessor to the person who holds that office after him.
