| Title        | Power of Parliament to legislate for two or more Provinces by consent.                      |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Added  | 14 August 1973                                                                              |
| Last Updated | 19 April 2010                                                                               |

(1) If one or more Provincial Assemblies pass resolutions to the effect that Parliament may by law regulate any matter not enumerated in the Federal Legislative List in the Fourth Schedule, it shall be lawful for Parliament to pass an Act for regulating that matter accordingly, but any Act so passed may, as respects any Province to which it applies, be amended or repealed by Act of the Assembly of that Province.
