# Objectives Resolution

**Era:** Dominion of Pakistan — the Interim Framework (1947–1956)  
**Date:** 1949-03-12  
**Type:** milestone

## Dossier

The Constituent Assembly's foundational statement of constitutional purpose, moved by Prime Minister Liaquat Ali Khan: sovereignty over the entire universe belongs to Allah, exercised by the people of Pakistan as a sacred trust, within democratic principles of freedom, equality, tolerance and social justice as enunciated by Islam.

## Key provisions and effects

- Sovereignty of Allah; authority a sacred trust exercised through the people
- Democracy, freedom, equality, tolerance and social justice as enunciated by Islam
- Muslims enabled to order their lives per Quran and Sunnah; minorities free to profess and practise their religions
- Independence of judiciary; federation with autonomous units; fundamental rights guaranteed

## How it ended

Prefaced every subsequent constitution (1956, 1962, 1973); made a substantive part of the 1973 Constitution as Article 2A by the Eighth Amendment (1985).
