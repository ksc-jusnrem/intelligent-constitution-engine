# Charter Act 1853

**Era:** Company Rule — Precursor Statutes (1773–1853)  
**Date:** 1853  
**Type:** precursor

## Dossier

The last charter renewal — and the first separation of legislative from executive business in India, foreshadowing a real legislature.

## Key provisions and effects

- Separate Legislative Council carved out of the Governor-General's Council
- Provincial representation in central legislation for the first time
- Civil service opened to competitive examination, ending Company patronage

## How it ended

Company rule itself ended five years later, after the 1857 uprising.
