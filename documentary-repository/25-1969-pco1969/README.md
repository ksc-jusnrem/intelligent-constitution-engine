# Provisional Constitution Order 1969

**Era:** Transition — From Martial Law to the 1973 Constitution (1969–1973)  
**Date:** 1969-04-04  
**Type:** order

## Dossier

Yahya Khan's bridge instrument: Pakistan governed 'as nearly as may be' in accordance with the abrogated 1962 Constitution, with fundamental rights and the courts' power to question martial law action suspended.

## Key provisions and effects

- 1962 Constitution as administrative template, without supremacy
- Courts barred from questioning martial law regulations
- All power derived from the 1969 proclamation

## How it ended

Superseded by the Interim Constitution of 1972.
