# Indian Councils Act 1892

**Era:** Crown Rule — Constitutional Frameworks of British India (1858–1947)  
**Date:** 1892  
**Type:** statute

## Dossier

Conceded the principle of indirect election in all but name and gave members the first real deliberative powers.

## Key provisions and effects

- Councils enlarged; bodies like municipalities, universities and chambers could recommend members
- Right to discuss the annual budget (without voting)
- Right to put questions to the executive

## How it ended

Superseded by the Morley–Minto reforms of 1909.
