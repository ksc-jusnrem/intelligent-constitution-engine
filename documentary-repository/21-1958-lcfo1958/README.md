# Laws (Continuance in Force) Order 1958

**Era:** The 1956 Constitution and the First Martial Law (1956–1962)  
**Date:** 1958-10-10  
**Type:** order

## Dossier

The military regime's bridge order: Pakistan would be governed 'as nearly as may be' in accordance with the abrogated constitution — minus its supremacy, with all power flowing from the martial law proclamation.

## Key provisions and effects

- Courts continued; writ jurisdiction preserved at the regime's sufferance
- Existing laws continued in force
- Template for every later extra-constitutional bridge order (1969, 1977, 1999)

## How it ended

Superseded by the 1962 Constitution.
