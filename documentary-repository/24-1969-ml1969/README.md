# Second Martial Law

**Era:** The 1962 Constitution and the Second Martial Law (1962–1969)  
**Date:** 1969-03-25  
**Type:** martial

## Dossier

Facing mass agitation in both wings through 1968–69, Ayub Khan resigned — bypassing the constitutional succession through the Speaker — and handed power to the army commander-in-chief, General Agha Muhammad Yahya Khan, who proclaimed martial law, abrogated the 1962 Constitution and dissolved the assemblies.

## Key provisions and effects

- Constitution abrogated; assemblies dissolved; Yahya as CMLA and (from 31 March 1969) President
- Extra-constitutional transfer later condemned as usurpation in Asma Jilani (1972)

## How it ended

The regime collapsed with the loss of East Pakistan; Yahya resigned on 20 December 1971.
